Our Deck Staining Process

1. Thorough Preparation

We begin with comprehensive deck preparation:

  • Professional power washing
  • Wood repair and replacement
  • Sanding rough areas
  • Nail/screw inspection and repair

2. Premium Materials

We use high-quality stains and sealers specifically designed for maximum durability and weather resistance.

3. Expert Application

Our skilled team applies stain using professional techniques to ensure even coverage and proper absorption.

4. Sealing and Protection

We apply appropriate sealers to protect against moisture, UV rays, and wear.
