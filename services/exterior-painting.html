Our Exterior Painting Process

1. Surface Preparation

We begin with thorough preparation to ensure long-lasting results:

  • Power washing to remove dirt and debris
  • Scraping and sanding loose paint
  • Repairing wood rot and damage
  • Caulking gaps and seams

2. Weather-Resistant Materials

We use premium exterior paints and materials designed to withstand harsh weather conditions and provide long-lasting protection.

3. Professional Application

Our skilled painters use industry-best techniques to ensure even coverage and proper paint thickness for maximum durability.

4. Site Clean-Up

We maintain a clean work site throughout the project and perform thorough clean-up upon completion.
